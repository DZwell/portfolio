About

When I started my journey down the path of software development after ten plus years as a professional musician, I was stunned to find how familiar the code writing process felt. The repetitive practice of writing for-loops over and over again to get the syntax down.


Jamming with my project teams to think of how we can efficiently solve the problem at hand. The same feelings of inspiration I get when hearing a great musician, I experience while reading someone else's code and think to my self, ‘that’s what I need to learn next’.


It’s almost as if my music career has prepared me for a life of software development, a life where creativity and logic come together, and a life that I’m excited to take to the next level and see how far I can go.
